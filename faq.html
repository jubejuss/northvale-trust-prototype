Our Funding

We support charities and CIOs with a national reach that meet our eligibility and carry out work within our categories. We don't fund work primarily for residents of London or Northern Ireland.
We prioritise charities with national reach that are agile, independent, well-run, and make a credible case for achieving intended outcomes.
The Trust was endowed in 1949 with a donation of over £2 million. Today, returns from investments of the endowment drive the Trust's grant making.

Our Application Process
