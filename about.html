About Us

An independent grant-making charity, founded in 1949.

The Northvale Trust was founded in 1949 by its founding benefactor, Sir Edmund Hartley.

Since its foundation, the Trust has made grants totalling over £120 million, providing much-needed support to thousands of charities.

Trustees

As a family foundation, the Trustees include members of the Founder's family as well as individuals with wide experience of the areas supported by the Trust.
