February Grants List

By Rhea Patel, Grants Manager

I'm in my third month at The Northvale Trust and, having just returned from two weeks away from the office, I've found myself reflecting on how much I've learned in such a short time.

This February, the Board awarded a new round of grants to charities working across our funding categories. As ever, we were struck by the quality, creativity and resilience of the organisations applying to us.

In this blog, I want to share a little about my first impressions of the Trust, the people I've met, and what excites me most about the work ahead.

We remain committed to supporting charities with flexibility and trust — offering unrestricted, multi-year funding wherever we can, so that our partners can focus on delivering impact.
