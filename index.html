Welcome to The Northvale Trust

We support UK charities with a national reach tackling a range of social issues, protecting the natural world, and preserving heritage crafts.

Our Categories

Youth Opportunities

Our largest category, supporting work with young people aged up to 25 on the basis of low income, building life skills to progress into education, employment or training.

General Welfare

Work in communities with high levels of poverty: family relationships, young offenders, homelessness, older people, carers and social cohesion.

Heritage

Developing craftsmanship in traditional techniques of repair and restoration, heritage skills training, and capital projects at UK cathedrals.

Conservation

Charities working on the health and resilience of the UK's wildlife habitat — native woodlands, coastal and marine environments.

Kenya and Uganda

Community development and wildlife conservation with charities that already have a long association with the Trust.

International Stability

Invitation-only category focused on disaster preparedness and peace intervention.

Applying for funding

We fund charities and CIOs with a national reach working within our funding categories. To apply, complete our eligibility quiz. If eligible, you'll receive a link to our online Expression of Interest form.
